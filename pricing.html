At ChargeShare, we believe charging should be for the people, by the people. Anyone will be able to invest in our charging network and receive benefits from it. Through your investment in the chargers, people throughout the United States will begin to see the ease of driving an electric vehicle.

Beginning Late October, any person of the general public will be able to invest in our chargers. Just sign up, decide how much you want to invest through our custom investment calculator, pay through our secure portal and wait for your benefits to hit your account dashboard. Investors can chose from a variety of benefits from a year of free fast charging to quarterly dividends. These benefits will depend on investment, location, and availability.

For any additional questions on investments, please visit our contact page.
