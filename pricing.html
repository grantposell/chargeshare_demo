Contribute

Interested in getting a financial return on a rapidly growing industry?

Not wanting to be directly invested but want to be a part of something big?

Through our entry level contributor program, you can chose to contribute from $100 up to $25,000. This program allows members to contribute through a portal and watch as their money grows. The investments made in this portfolio will go towards securing more charging stations and deploying them across the United States. Through usage of the charging networks and other supplemental income streams, dividends from your contribution will arrive through your portal. To calculate your return, below we have a calculator that will help get an idea of a possible amount you could expect. Finally, all contributors will receive emails on exclusive news before the public.

For more direct information, feel free to get in touch with us through our contact page.

Still not sure? Keep up to date with our blog or stay connected with us through email updates.
