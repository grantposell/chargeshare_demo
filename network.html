The Network

ChargeShare is fully committed to driving the adoption of electric vehicles through fast and affordable charging solutions. Our chargers will be located within cities as well as high-speed corridors to accommodate different situations. Our customers will be able to take advantage of amenities such as shopping, parks and other points of interest. Our open investor position platform helps accelerate this drive towards electric mobility and we hope you join us in our endeavors. For more information on our network, please visit our contact page. We would be happy to assist you.
