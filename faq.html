FAQ

What is ChargeShare?

ChargeShare is a company that is fully committed to driving the adoption of electric vehicles through providing fast and reliable charging infrastructure.
The charging infrastructure will be centered around our mission to use fully renewable and sustainable energy.

What makes ChargeShare different from other charging networks?

ChargeShare has many differentiators from other networks. ChargeShare is the only network in the United States that will be able to charge any vehicle made by any manufacturer.
Secondly, our network is open to investments from anyone. We are a charging network that is for the people, by the people.

What cars are able to charge at ChargeShare stations?

Any car sold in the United States will be able to charge at a ChargeShare station. To see what plug type is suited to your vehicle, visit our charging guide.

Where can I find a charging station?

Our network is rapidly expanding and always changing. Chargers can be found using our network page.

How long does it take to charge?

Excellent question! Charging varies based on vehicle, state of charge in the vehicle, and charging station equipment.
For closer estimates on charging times, visit our charging guide.

Can I charge without having a membership?

Certainly, our charging stations are easy to use with our conveniently placed credit card readers. Rates vary based on location of charging and amount charged. If you change your mind, you can review memberships at our invest page.

How much is it to charge?

Charging rates can be found on our invest page. Rates will vary based on state and amount charged.

What memberships are available?

Membership plans can be found on our invest page.
Rates will vary based on state and amount charged.

How do I sign up for an account?

In the top left side of every page, there is a signup/login button. Otherwise, you can click this link and it will take you to signup/login.

What is a contributor vs an Investor?

A contributor is someone who wants to invest below $7,000 and only reaps financial benefit from the investment. Investors are people who invest over $7,000 and are eligible to select locations for network. Locations pend approval and financial amount given.

What is the difference between an Investor and a Partnership?

A Partnership is between two parties of mutual agreement to join in a deal to place chargers in favorable spots to the partner. Partners will be organizations, companies, and government entities. Investors and Partnerships can give similar financial backings to ChargeShare but Partnerships are facilitated on a customized basis.
